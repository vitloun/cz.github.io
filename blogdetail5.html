How to prepare delicious
Chicken tender



Ingredients

Here's a simple recipe for crispy and delicious chicken tenders that can be either fried, baked, or cooked in an air fryer.

  • 1 ib (450g) chicken tenders (or boneless, skinless chicken breasts cut into strips)
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper , to taste
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups breadcrumbs (Panko breadcrumbs for extra crunch)
  • Cooking oil(for frying, or spray oil for baking/air frying)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Breading Station:
    • Flour mixture: In a shallow bowl, mix the flour, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, salt, and pepper.
    • Egg wash: In another boel, whink together the eggs and 1 tablespoon of water.
    • Breadcrumbs: Place the breadcrumbs in a third bowl. You can also season the breadcrumbs with more paprika, garlic powder, or even Parmesan cheese for added flavor.
  2. Coast the chicken:
    • Step 1: Pat the chicken tenders dry with paper towels to help the coating stick.
    • Step 2: Dredge each chicken tender in the seasoned flour, shaking off any excess.
    • Step 3: Dip the floured chicken into the egg wash coating it completely.
    • Step 4: Finally, press the chiken tenders into the breadcrumbs, ensuring they are well-casted. Set aside on a plate.


Cooking Methods:

  1. Frying Method:
    • Heat oil: Pour about ½ inch of oil into a large skillet and heat over medium-high heat until the oil reaches about 350°F (175°C). You can test with a small piece of bread—if it sizzles, the oil is ready.
    • Fry the chicken: Add the chicken tenders to the pan in batches (don’t overcrowd the pan) and fry for about 3–4 minutes on each side, or until golden brown and fully cooked (internal temperature should reach 165°F/74°C).
    • Drain: Remove the tenders and drain them on a plate lined with paper towels to absorb excess oil.
  2. Baking Method:
    • Prepare oven: Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
    • Prepare baking sheet: Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or lightly grease it with cooking spray.
    • Bake the tenders: Place the breaded chicken tenders on the prepared baking sheet and lightly spray them with cooking oil (this helps them crisp up).
    • Bake: Bake for about 15–20 minutes, flipping halfway through, until golden brown and cooked through (internal temp 165°F/74°C).
  3. Air Frying Method:
    • Prepare air fryer: Preheat the air fryer to 375°F (190°C) for about 3 minutes.
    • Air fry the tender: Place the chicken tenders in a single layer in the air fryer basket. Lightly spray with cooking oil.
    • Cook: Air fry for 10–12 minutes, flipping halfway through, until they’re crispy and golden brown (internal temp 165°F/74°C).

Serving Suggestions:

  • Serve the chicken tenders with dipping sauces like ketchup, ranch dressing, BBQ sauce, or honey mustard.
  • Pair them with fries, a salad, or roasted vegetables for a complete meal.
