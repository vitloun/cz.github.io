How to make
Strawberry Smoothie



Here's a simple recipe for the delicious Strawberry Smoothie

Ingredient

  • 1 cup fresh or frozen strawberries
  • 1/2 cup yogurt (vanilla or plain)
  • 1/2 cup milk (or almond milk for a dairy-free option)
  • 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup (optional, for added sweetness)
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)
  • A few ice cubes (if using fresh strawberries)

Instructions

  • Prepare the Ingredients: If you're using fresh stawberries, wash and hull them (remove the steams).
  • Blend: Add the strawberies, yogurt, milk, honey (if using), and vanilla extract to a blender. Blend until smooth.
  • Add ice: If you prefer a thicker smoothie, add a few ice cubes and blend again until fully combined.
  • Serve: Pour the smoothie into a glass and enjoy immediately!
