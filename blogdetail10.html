How to prepare a delicious
Chicken Curry



Here's a simple recipe for the delicious Chicken Curry

Ingredient

  • 500g chicken
  • 2 onions
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 2-3 garlic cloves
  • 1 inch ginger
  • 2 green chilies
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1-2 tbsp cooking oil
  • Fresh coriander

Spices:

  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 2 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt (to taste)

Instructions

  1. Marinate the chicken (Optional):
    • Mix chicken with yogurt, 1/2 tsp turmeric, 1 tsp red chili powder, and a pich of salt. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes. This step will tenderize the chicken.
  2. Prepare the base:
    • Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and bay leaf. Once they splutter, add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
    • Add minced garlic, ginger, and green chilies. Cook until the raw smell disappears (about 1-2 minutes).
  3. Add tomatoes and spices:
    • Add the chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft and oil separates from the mixture.
    • Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, and cumin powder. Cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  4. Cook the chicken:
    • Add the marinated or fresh chicken pieces to the pan. Stir to coat the chicken with the spice mixture.
    • Cover and cook for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through.
  5. Add water for gravy:
    • Add about 1 cup of water (more if you prefer more gravy) and bring it to a boil.
    • Reduce the heat and let it simmer for another 10 minutes until the gravy thickens.
  6. Final touch:
    • Add garam masala and mix well. Cook for another 2-3 minutes.
    • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
