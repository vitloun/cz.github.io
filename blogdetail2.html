The secret tips & tricks to prepare a
fresh Sushi for our customers



Ingredients

  1. For sushi rice: 2 cups sushi rice (short-grain rice) 2 ¼ cups water ¼ cup rice vinegar 2 tbsp sugar.
  2. For fillings: Fresh fish (e.g., tuna, salmon) or cooked shrimp, Vegetables (e.g., cucumber, avocado, carrots), Gluten-free imitation crab (if using), Cream cheese (optional, ensure it’s gluten-free)
  3. For wrapping: Gluten-free nori sheets (check for a gluten-free label)
  4. For dipping & garnishing: Gluten-free soy sauce (tamari) Pickled ginger (gluten-free) Wasabi (make sure it’s gluten-free)

Instructions

  • Prepare the sushi rice: Rince the rice in cold water until the water runs clear to remove excess starch. Add the rice and water to a rice cooker or pot. Cook according to the rice cooker's instructions, or bring the rice to a boil in thr pot, then reduce the heat and left sit for 10 minutes. In a small saucepan, heat the rice vinegar, sugar, and salt until dissolved(do not boil). Stir this mixture into the cooked rice and let the rice cool to room temperature.
  • Prepare your fillings: Slice your vegetables and fish into strips for easy rolling.
  • Assemble the sushi: Lay a sheet of nori (shiny side down) on a bamboo sushi mat (you can also use a towel covered with plastic wrap). Wet your hands to prevent the rice from sticking, then spread a thin layer of sishi rice evenly over the nori, leaving a 1 inch(2.5 cm) strip at the top edge. Lay your fish and vegetable strips horizontally across the center of the rice. Roll the sushi tightly, using the bamboo mat to help. Wet the exposed edge of the nori and press to seal the roll. Cut the sushi roll into 6-8 pieces using a sharp, wet knite.
  • Serve: Arrage the sushi on a plate and serve with gluten-free tamari, pickled ginger, and wasabi, etc.
