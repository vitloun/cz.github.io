How to make Fresh
Orange Juice

Here's how to make fresh orange juice for your family and your love:

Ingredient

  • 4-5 ripe orange (or more, depending on how much juice you want)
  • Opitional: sugar or honey for sweetnees
  • Optional: ice cubes or cold water

Equipment:

  • A juicer (manual or electric) or a blender
  • A strainer (optional if you want pulp-free juice)
  • A kinfe

Instructions

  1. Wash the oranges: Rinse them thoroughly under cool water to remove any dirt or residue.
  2. Cut the oranges: Slice the oranges in half. If you're using a blender, peel them and remove as much of the white pith as possible.
  3. Juicing:
    • Juicing with a juicer:
      Manual Juicer: Press each orange half on the juicer, twist it to extract the juice.
      Electric Juicer: Just press the oranges onto the juicer cone, and let it spin out the juice.
    • Using a blender:
      Peel the orange and separate them into segments.
      Place them into the blender and blend until smooth.
      If you want pulp-free juice, pour the mixture through a fine mesh strainer into a jug to remove the pulp.
  4. Sweeten (optional): If the juice tastes a little too tart, you can add sugar, honey, or your preferred sweetener.
  5. Serve: Add ice cubes or chill the juice in the fridge before serving.
