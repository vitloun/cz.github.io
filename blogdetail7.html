How to prepare delicious
Pizza for Our Customer



Preparing the perfect pizza requires attention to the dough, sauce, toppings, and baking technique. Here’s a step-by-step guide to help you achieve pizza perfection:

Ingredient

For the Dough:

  • 500g all flour
  • 320ml warm water
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 7g active dry yeast (or 10g fresh yeast)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

For the Tomatoes Sauce:

  • 400g canned crushed tomatoes
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves

For the Toppings:

  • Fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil for drizzling
  • Your choise of toppings (e.g. pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers)

Step by Step Instructions:

  1. Choose the right :
    • Opt for steaks about 1-1.5 inches thick, with good marbling marbling (fat distribution) for tenderness and flavor.
  2. Prepare the dough:
    • Mix: in a bowl, dissolve the yeast and sugar in warm water. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until it bacomes frothy.
    • Combine: In aother bowl, mix the flour and salt. Add the yeast mixture and olive oil to the flour mixture. Mix until a dough forms.
    • Knead: Transfer the dough to a lightly floured surface and knead for about 8-10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
    • Rise: Place the dough in an oiled bowl, cover with a dramp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for 1-2 hours or until doubled in size.
  3. Prepare the Tomato Sauce:
    • Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
    • Add minced garlic and saute until fragrant (about 1 minute).
    • Add crushed tomatoes, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine.
    • Add fresh basil leaves at the end. Let it cool.
  4. Shape the Dough:
    • Punch down the dough to release air.
    • Divide the dough into 2-4 equal portions, depending on the size.
    • Shape the dough into balls and then flatten each ball into a disk using a rolling pin or your hands.
  5. Assemble the Pizza:
    • Transfer the stretched dough to a pizza peel or a baking sheet lined with parchment paper if you don’t have a pizza stone.
    • Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce on the dough, leaving a small border for the crust.
    • Add sliced mozzarella cheese and any other toppings of your choice.
    • Drizzle a little olive oil over the top.
  6. Bake the Pizza:
    • Sliced the pizza onto the hot pizza stone or directly into the oven.
    • Bake for 8-12 minutes or until the crust is ggolden and the chesse is bubbly and slightly browned.
  7. Finish and Serve:
    • Remove from the oven and add fresh basil leaves on top.
    • Slice and serve hot!


Tips for the delicious Pizza:

  • Dough rest: If you have time, let the dough rest in the fridge overnight for the better flavor.
  • High heat: The hotter the oven, the cripier the crust. A pizza stone or steel will hepl simulate a pizzeria-style oven.
  • Fresh ingredients: Use high-quality mozzarella, tomatoes, and fresh herbs for the best flavor.
